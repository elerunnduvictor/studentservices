Dean of Students

Our Purpose
Mission Statement
“

The Dean of Students office serves as a central advocate for students, fostering a culture of belonging, integrity, and well-being across the entire student body.

We uphold community standards through fair and educational conduct processes, ensure accessibility for all learners, address grievances with care and impartiality, and provide critical crisis support when students need it most.

What We Do
Key Focus Areas
Honor & Conduct
Upholding academic integrity and community standards through fair, educational conduct processes.
Accessibility Services

Belonging & Inclusion
Creating welcoming spaces and programs that foster a sense of community and belonging for all students.
Crisis Support
Providing timely intervention, resources, and guidance for students experiencing personal or academic crises.
