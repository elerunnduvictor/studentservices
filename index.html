Message from Leadership March 2026
Serving with Purpose

We want to remind you of the incredible impact you have on students' lives every single day. Each support ticket resolved, each enrollment guided, every process improved — these are moments where we help someone take one more step toward their future. We are reaching out to the hidden many and inviting them to step into the light of Christ.

Look for the student behind every case number. Find one thing you can do better than before. Remember that the work we do here matters more than we can easily see from behind the hustle and bustle of our daily tasks.

We are grateful for each of you and the dedication you bring.
